Lasagna

  • Prep time: 30mins
  • Cook time: 2hrs 30mins
  • Additional time: 15mins
  • Total Time: 3hrs15mins
  • Servings: 12

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest
